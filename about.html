About us

Formed in 1997, London Running Club is a well established running club, well equipped to cater for runners and joggers of all abilities. Our home is The Pavilion Sports and Leisure Club in Eltham where we meet up every Wednesday and Friday evening for a prompt 7pm start. Every session has a variety of distances/paces on offer (including walking) and we like to mix our running schedules with regular fitness evenings from our qualified coach. On top of this, if you like to take in the Kent country side, then why not join us for a nice social Sunday morning run! In addition to our running, we have a comprehensive programme of social activities throughout the year. Please feel free to come and join us for a run, we will ensure you are looked after no matter what your ability level !

About Us

We welcome everyone from absolute beginners to seasoned marathon runners. Nothing beats the endorphin rush after a run and the joy of meeting like minded people with a common interest so what’s stopping you… come and join the fun!

To help you achieve your goals we have a large number of coaches and run leaders helping out with our beginners programme and our structured sessions. Following one of the schedules our coaches have put together is a great way to improve as a runner.

Training

The club has a large range of training options throughout the week: coached on- and off-track sessions and uncoached sessions at several venues, and various social club runs. For experienced athletes, as well as high-performance coaching, many faster members meet for group training on Thursdays and Sundays.
